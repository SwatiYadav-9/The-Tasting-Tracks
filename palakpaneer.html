Palak Paneer Recipe

Ingredients :

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 bunches spinach (palak), cleaned and chopped
  • 2 onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies (chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee

Instructions :

  1. Blanch the spinach in boiling water for 2 minutes, then blend into a smooth paste.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, and sauté for another minute.
  4. Add tomato puree, turmeric, red chili powder, and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Add the spinach puree and cook for another 5 minutes.
  6. Add paneer cubes and garam masala, and simmer for 3-4 minutes.
  7. Stir in cream and salt, and cook for 2 minutes.
  8. Serve hot with naan or rice.
