Samosa Recipe

Ingredients :

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4-5 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1 cup peas (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed)
  • Oil (for frying)

Instructions :

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, a pinch of salt, and 2 tablespoons of oil. Gradually add water to form a dough. Cover and let it rest for 15-20 minutes.
  2. For the filling, heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, ginger-garlic paste, and saut√© for a minute.
  3. Add mashed potatoes, peas, turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well and cook for 5 minutes. Set aside to cool.
  4. Divide the dough into small balls and roll them into thin circles. Cut each circle in half to form two semi-circles.
  5. Fold each semi-circle into a cone, fill with the potato mixture, and seal the edges.
  6. Heat oil in a pan and deep fry the samosas until golden brown.
  7. Serve hot with chutney.
