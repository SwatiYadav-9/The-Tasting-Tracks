Gulab Jamun Recipe

Ingredients :

  • 1 cup khoya (milk solids)
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon rose water
  • 4-5 cardamom pods (crushed)
  • Oil or ghee for frying

Instructions :

  1. In a bowl, mix khoya, maida, and baking soda to form a smooth dough. Divide into small balls.
  2. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry the balls on low heat until golden brown.
  3. In another pan, make sugar syrup by dissolving sugar in water and adding crushed cardamom and rose water. Simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Soak the fried gulab jamun in warm sugar syrup for at least 2 hours.
  5. Serve warm or at room temperature.
