Spicy Pav Bhaji Recipe

Ingredients :

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 1 cup green peas
  • 1 cup cauliflower florets
  • 1 cup potatoes, cubed
  • 1/2 cup carrots, diced
  • 1/2 cup capsicum, diced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 cup water
  • 4 pav buns
  • Butter for grilling the pav

Instructions :

  1. Heat butter in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion, garlic, and ginger. Saut√© until softened.
  3. Add green peas, cauliflower, potatoes, carrots, and capsicum. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  4. Stir in cumin powder, garam masala, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add water and simmer until the vegetables are cooked and the sauce thickens.
  6. Grill the pav buns with butter on a griddle or pan.
  7. Serve the pav bhaji with the grilled pav buns. Garnish with chopped onions, cilantro, and a squeeze of lemon juice.
